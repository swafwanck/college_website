Learn About JNE

In an era , when Muslim community along with other marginalized backward communities continued to exist in illiteracy and ignorance , Jamia Nadwiyya laid its foundation in 1964 by Kerala  Nadvathul Mujahideen (KNM) a socio- cultural – educational renaissance movement in Kerala . The vision of Jamia Nadwiyya is to develop into a NAAC accredited institution having international standards by moulding proficient academicians and professionals with creative idea, brilliant practical knowledge and excellent communication skills upholding moral values. The mission of Jamia Nadwiyya is to facilitate graduate and post graduate studies in Arabic language and literature, social science and modern science as well as to provide academic atmosphere for teaching, learning and doing research in an array of fields by recognizing the needs and challenges of the times. Jamia Nadwiyya was started with the Sharia college offering various B A and M A courses later offered hadith institute. JN Arts and Science College imparts modern education along with moral and spiritual values. Now Jamia Nadwiyya have many another institutions. Thank You
